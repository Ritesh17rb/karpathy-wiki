Synopsis

In this section, we will review the microbiota found in a healthy digestive tract and the general signs and symptoms associated with oral and GI infections. General Signs and Symptoms of Oral and GI Disease Despite numerous defense mechanisms that protect against infection, all parts of the digestive tract can become sites of infection or intoxication. The term food poisoning is sometimes used as a catch-all for GI infections and intoxications, but not all forms of GI disease originate with foodborne pathogens or toxins.
