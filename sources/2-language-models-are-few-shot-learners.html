Language Models are Few-Shot Learners

Language Models are Few-Shot Learners Tom B. We further specialize the description to “zero-shot”, “one-shot”, or “few-shot” depending on how many demonstrations are provided at inference time. Few-shot learning also improves dramatically with model size. For example, GPT-3 achieves 81.5 F1 on CoQA in the zero-shot setting, 84.0 F1 on CoQA in the one-shot setting, 85.0 F1 in the few-shot setting.
